The Onyx Apartments.


Our current project.
Modern and luxurious 64 apartment homes. Minds and hearts have come together to develop this stunning new home, just FOR YOU.

  • Design is not monotonous, alongside the stunning details, we provide an SQ option, open/closed kitchens.
  • The apartments are located off waiyaki way along mukiri road. Approximately 12km to the Nairobi CBD, accessibility and social amenities are guaranteed. Enjoy the secure & secluded space.
  • These are 3 bedroom apartments, 32 with DSQ & 32 without DSQ.

Completion of project is expected from JULY - AUG 2021.

Family-oriented homes but with a taste of class, a home to last you for decades.

04

Security

24 hr CCTV surveillance, electric fence and a lovely small community. For water and electricity needs, a standby generator & borehole.

Bank Guarantee
